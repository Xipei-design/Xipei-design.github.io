Perswedo cards support designers access to persuasive technology principles and leverage such theories in design practices.

The first iteration of Perswedo.

The second iteration of Perswedo.

Perswedo contains four categories of 28 design cards (7 cards per category).

We explored blending Perswedo into studio-based learning. Persuasive technology became a new dimension of service design education.

For more detail of Perswedo:

Ren, X., Lu, Y., Oinas-Kukkonen, H., and Brombacher, A. Perswedo: introducing persuasive principles into the creative design process through a design card-set. In IFIP conference on human-computer interaction (INTERACT), Springer, 2017. [ pdf ]
